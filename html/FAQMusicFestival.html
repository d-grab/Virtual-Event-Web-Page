We have put together some FAQs for you that should help answer any queries you may have.

How Can I get access to The Rivers Music Festival ?

After making a payment, You will get the link for the ZOOM meeting. That link will be active on the day of the event. Only what You need is access to the internet. You will get your username and password, which You will use for login purposes.

Can I access The Festival from my mobile phone ?

Yes !!! . You can access it from any internet browser and any mobile device, only what You need is a stable internet connection. Preferable is a device with bigger screen for better impression

Can I access an event from one account simultaneously on two different devices ? ?

Unfortunately not. You can log in only to one device from the same account. If You want to get access to two devices, You need to buy another ticket to the Music Festival.

Can I access two channels in the same time ?

You can access only one channel at time. It will be an option to buy record from any stages, becaouse all artist will be recorded.

What if I can not access the Festival (The link is not working) ?. Can I get a refund?

We provide online chat and video calls to help with access to The Music Festival. If You are not happy with your purchase, You can get a refund until 12 hours after your purchase.

Can I get access only for the artist I would like to see, or do I have to obtain access for all events?

Yes. There are many options of how can You get access to the event. You can get access only for one stage, channel or only for some time. It is up to You how much You would like to see and how much You would like to pay. All of the details will be provided in the email after the registration.

Sponsors
